Find A Court

Overview

For a personal project, I designed an app for finding public tennis courts in Palo Alto.

The Problem

As an avid tennis player and former Palo Alto Senior High School Varisty Tennis Team Captain, I'm a frequent user of Palo Alto public tennis courts. Me and my teammates noticed the courts were often full and there was no centralized place to report tennis wait times or find tennis courts to begin with.

The Solution

Find A Court provides users with the latest information on the 10 public tennis court facilities in Palo Alto. Users can see which court is closest to them and look at court features (number of courts, restroom access, noise rating, and parking availability). Additionally, users can submit data and look at the up to date court availability graph for each facility.

Tools and Skills

Figma | UI/UX Design, Wireframing, Prototyping

Next Steps

In the future I want to make a page for each facility to make my design a working prototype. Additionally, I would want to figure out whether users would find any other features useful, and then add those to my prototype accordingly.
